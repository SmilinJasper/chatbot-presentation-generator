#Slide: 2
#Header: Introduction to Cats
#Content: 
- Cats are small, carnivorous mammals.
- They are often kept as pets and are known for their independent nature.
- Cats have been domesticated for thousands of years and are one of the most popular pets worldwide.

#Slide: 3
#Header: Characteristics of Cats
#Content: 
- Cats have sharp retractable claws and are excellent climbers.
- They have highly developed senses, including excellent night vision and hearing.
- Cats are known for their agility and flexibility, which allows them to squeeze through small spaces.

#Slide: 4
#Header: Characteristics of Cats (cont.)
#Content: 
- Cats are known for their grooming habits and spend a significant amount of time cleaning themselves.
- They have a unique hunting instinct and are skilled predators.
- Cats communicate through vocalizations, body language, and scent marking.

#Slide: 5
#Header: Summary
#Content: 
- Cats are small, carnivorous mammals that have been domesticated for thousands of years.
- They have sharp claws, excellent senses, and are known for their agility.
- Cats are skilled predators and communicate through various means.
