#Title: What is a Whale?

#Slide: 1
#Header: What is a Whale?
#Content: 
- Whales are marine mammals that belong to the order Cetacea.
- They are the largest animals on Earth.
- Whales have streamlined bodies, a blowhole on top of their head, and a tail fin called a fluke.
- They are known for their ability to communicate through songs and their complex social structures.
- Whales are found in oceans all around the world and come in various species, including the blue whale, humpback whale, and killer whale.
