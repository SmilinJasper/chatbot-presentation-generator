#Title: DEFINING CATS

#Slide: 1
#Header: Defining Cats
#Content: 
- Cats are small, carnivorous mammals.
- They are domesticated pets and are often kept as companions.
- Cats have retractable claws and are known for their agility and hunting skills.
- They come in various breeds, colors, and sizes.
- Cats are known for their independent nature and ability to clean themselves.
